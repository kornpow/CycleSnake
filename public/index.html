CycleSnake

A Web Game for Everyone

Follow Me

Studies have reported that commuters who have social interactions during their commute experience increased happiness

The World Outside

For children who are stuck in hospitals, there is a lack of everyday social interaction that they don't get to experience due to their confines

Satisfaction

CycleSnake works to satisfy the need for social interaction that is present in today's world, where we are constantly far too busy

Responsive

CycleSnake leverages socket.io, express.js and node.js to deliver real-time bidirectional gameplay. Hosted on Cycle, CycleSnake has some of the most efficient node.js Docker images on the planet

.

Local Impact

You're not only curing your boredom while playing CycleSnake; you're also helping to make the lives of those who lack mobility and the ability to interact socially an outlet to do so.

Right in your Browser

CycleSnake was developed as a web application which means that it's easy for you to access the game given a computer and internet connection

Check out our Collaborative and One vs. One Snake game modes
